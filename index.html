Executive Directive 5105-A

Text of Directive:

EXECUTIVE DIRECTIVE 5105-A

EFFECTIVE: IMMEDIATELY

Pursuant to Article II powers and Section 42 of the National Continuity Framework, the President hereby enacts full federal continuity under Emergency Continuity Protocol Alpha (ECPA).

1. Civilian federal authorities are hereby superseded by the Crisis Continuity Authority (CCA), operating under Department of Homeland Security command and Presidential oversight.
2. Deployment of federal military units and intelligence assets is authorized in all domestic zones exhibiting signs of disruption, sedition, or open revolt.
3. Constitutionally guaranteed rights, including habeas corpus, assembly, and press freedoms, are hereby suspended in affected regions pending resolution of national threat indicators.
4. National broadcast, communications, and transportation networks are under temporary federal command to ensure continuity of message, logistics, and emergency response.
5. All state governors and local officials are ordered to coordinate with Federal Regional Authorities or face classification under Section 12: Non-Cooperative Entity.

This directive remains in effect until expressly revoked in writing by Presidential or Congressional authority.

— Veronica Aldridge, President of the United States
